Every prospect gets a Sourcing Strain Score from 0 to 100
The Analyst takes each signal and measures it against the Sourcing Strain Index — a scoring model with six dimensions. Higher scores mean the company is under more pressure and needs expert help more urgently. Scores update in real time as new signals arrive.
The Six Dimensions
  • Vendor UniverseHow many vendors must they evaluate?
  • Eval ComplexityHow complicated is the buying process?
  • Expertise GapDo they have specialists on staff?
  • Timeline PressureHow soon must they decide?
  • Compliance BurdenWhat regulations apply?
  • StakeholdersHow many decision-makers involved?
Priority Tiers
HOT Score 75+ — Needs help now. Active evaluation underway.
WARM Score 40-74 — Growing pressure. Engagement window opening.
NURTURE Score 20-39 — Early signals. Worth monitoring.
STRATEGIC Under 20 — Long-term opportunity. Not urgent yet.
Example
"Meridian Health scores 82 (HOT) — Avaya end-of-life forcing migration, board-mandated AI strategy by Q3, HIPAA compliance requirements."

The system gets smarter with every outcome
The Monitor closes the loop. It tracks what happened with each prospect — did they engage? Did the expert match work? Did the deal close? These outcomes feed back into the scoring model, so predictions get more accurate over time. Every quarter, the model recalibrates itself.
